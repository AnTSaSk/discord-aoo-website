## What is Albion Online Objectives?

Albion Online Objectives (AOO) helps Albion Online guilds turn scattered Black Zone intelligence into coordinated action. Guild members report objectives they find — resource nodes, energy cores, and vortexes — and the bot keeps a shared, always-updated list for the entire server.

Unlike bots that scrape game data, Albion Online Objectives relies entirely on **manual reports** from guild members. This makes it lightweight, compliant with game rules, and independent of any external API.

## Why Albion Online Objectives?

Black Zone objectives in Albion Online are time-limited events spread across hundreds of maps. Without coordination, guilds miss valuable spawns or waste time on objectives that have already expired. Albion Online Objectives solves this by providing a simple, shared tracking system directly in Discord — where guilds already communicate.

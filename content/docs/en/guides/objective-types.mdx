Black Zone objectives in Albion Online are timed open-world events that spawn valuable resources. Albion Online Objectives (AOO) tracks three categories: **resource nodes**, **energy cores**, and **vortexes**.

This page lists every type and rarity supported by the [`/add`](/docs/commands/add/) command.

## Resource Nodes

Resource nodes spawn gatherable materials in the Black Zone. Each node type corresponds to a crafting resource. Nodes use **tier-based rarities** from <Badge variant="tier">4.4</Badge> to <Badge variant="tier">8.4</Badge> — higher tiers yield rarer materials.

## Energy Cores

Energy cores are powerful objectives that provide Fame and Silver rewards. They use **color-based rarities**: <Badge variant="green">Green</Badge> <Badge variant="blue">Blue</Badge> <Badge variant="purple">Purple</Badge> <Badge variant="gold">Gold</Badge>.

## Vortexes

Vortexes are open-world objectives that offer rewards similar to cores, with their own spawn patterns. They use the same **color-based rarities**: <Badge variant="green">Green</Badge> <Badge variant="blue">Blue</Badge> <Badge variant="purple">Purple</Badge> <Badge variant="gold">Gold</Badge>.
